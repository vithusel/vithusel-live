Retro Console

This project began as more of a time pass. I saw software called Retropie availible on the internet and once viewing this i felt a need to implement it within a old console. The choice of console for this was a Playstation 1. I used parts of the main powersupply but scrapped everything else internally including remodelling the plastic within the housing so i could fit a Pi 3B + with enough ventilation. I had the original controller ports rewired and connected to a makeshift usb adapter so that the software can stil understand input from original controllers and had the boot splashed changed to mimic that of a PS1. Have a look through the above pictures to see my progress.
